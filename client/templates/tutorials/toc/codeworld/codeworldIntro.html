Algorithm Complexity

Algorithm complexity is a mathematical representation of the complexity of a given algorithmic function. Algorithm efficiency is measured in notations such as Big-Oh.

Algorithmic function complexity should be written in italics as follows:

Ο(n) - Standard Big-Oh notation representing the upper bound of ƒ(n)

Θ(n) - Theta notation, representing the lower bound of ƒ(n)

o(n) - Little-Oh notation, representing the upper bound but not equal to ƒ(n) (approximation)

Ω(n) - Omega notation, representing the lower bound of ƒ(n)

For the sake of brevity only Big-Oh notation is applied.
